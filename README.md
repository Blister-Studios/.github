# Welcome to Blister Studios

We are an independent game development studio dedicated to building high-speed, mechanically tight, and visually striking games. We believe in building custom tools, iterating fast, and working closely with our community to make games that feel amazing to play.

### What We're Building

**RiftRunner** Our current flagship project is *RiftRunner*, a blazing-fast, dimension-shifting platformer. Players must seamlessly swap between Light and Dark dimensions mid-air to phase through walls, trigger platforms, and maintain their kinetic momentum.
